Alumni

A GALAXY OF SUCCESSFUL ALUMNI

The 5000 strong Alumni Body constitutes a formidable force which Mayo Boys can tap into when in School or even after they have completed class 12.

A GALAXY OF SUCCESSFUL ALUMNI

Spread across the world, Mayoites share a strong bond with the school and with each other. Alumni chapters are active in many countries. Old boys keep returning to the school and contribute whole heartedly to their Alma Mater. They participate in the ‘Old Boys Lecture Series’ and ‘Distinguished Speaker Series’. They conduct workshops for students and provide internships for the seniors through the Mayo Intern Cell. They engage with the students, invest their time, knowledge and also contribute financially for the development of Mayo College. They are our best brand ambassadors and do us proud by excelling in their respective fields. The fact that Mayo College remains close to their hearts is evident by the fact that many of them from across the world attend DÉJÀ VU. An annual 3 day winter event on campus where batchmates, their wives and children get together to relive past experiences and foster the spirit called Mayo. Alumni also come to Mayo every year to celebrate MARS. What began as Mayo Alumni Reunion Soccer has now become a full fledged Sports event which results in great bonding. We have had families with five/six generations passing out of Mayo.

"My years at Mayo were instrumental in making me what I am. Mayo ensures all round development of personality and character. It provides holistic education whether in the classroom or on the playing field. Also, one makes life long friends."

Admiral Sunil Lanba

Former Indian Navy Chief
