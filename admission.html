Admission Details

INFORMATION ABOUT ADMISSIONS

Common Aptitude Analysis for admission to Mayo College is held in the 3rd week of November, every year, at various centers in India. It can also be arranged overseas through the India Embassy in that country. The Analysis evaluates the candidate in English, Hindi and Mathematics to test the age specified skills. Parents are notified in the month of August preceding the year in which admission is sought. Only those students who are eligible as per their previous Class of study and who are registered for admission are allowed to take the Aptitude Analysis.

CAA Syllabus

At present the senior school has classes 7 to 12, and the junior school from class 4 to 6. The boys take the class 10 examination at the age of 15 and class 12 examinations at the age of 17. The medium of instruction is English but the school lays equally a strong emphasis on Hindi too. The subjects taught in the normal curriculum are : Hindi, English, Sanskrit, Punjabi, Gujarati, French, Geography, History, Civics, Economics, Commerce, Maths, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Psychology, Career Counseling, Music,Automobile Engineering, Woodwork, Metal work, Book craft, Computer studies and fine arts.
